Our Story

Maritide Hotel was established in June 2019 with the mission of creating a space where comfort meets productivity. Located in the heart of Nairobi, we cater to both leisure guests and professionals who value reliable hospitality.

Our Mission

To provide WCH services that combine modern amenities with the warmth of Kenyan hospitality. Our guests enjoy ergonomic rooms, coworking spaces, and 24/7 concierge support.

Our Values

  • Comfort: Spacious rooms with contemporary design.
  • Productivity: High-speed Wi-Fi and tech-ready lounges.
  • Excellence: Dedicated staff trained to exceed expectations.
